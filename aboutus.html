Who We Are

Growvelt Technologies is a data-focused tutoring company founded in 2024. We're on a mission to bridge the skill gap in tech by offering hands-on, career-oriented training in data analytics and beyond.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading tutoring brand, producing exceptional talent with global impact in tech and data.


To be a leading technology hub where innovation meets practical learning, helping individuals and businesses unlock their full potential in the digital era.


Our mission

Equip learners with industry-relevant skills, provide hands-on experiences, and deliver customized solutions that help organizations leverage technology and data to drive growth.

What We Offer

  • Real-world projects & mentorship
  • Career Consultation & job support
  • Supportive, beginner-friendly teaching
  • Remote & onsite learning options

Why Choose Growvelt?

Our experienced instructors, hands-on learning approach, flexible training models, and proven track record ensure that you receive the best tech education possible. We prepare you for success in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.
